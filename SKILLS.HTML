MY SKILLS

WEB DEVELOPMENT COURSE BY NSDC AND UDEMY

THIS COURSE IS DESIGNED TO PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE TRAINING IN WEB DEVELOPMENT, COVERING BOTH FRONT END AND BACK END TECHNOLOGIES. PARTICIPANTS WILL LEARN HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT FOR CREATING INTERACTIVE AND VISUALLY APPEALING WEBSITES. THEY WILL ALSO DELVE INTO SERVER-SIDE SCRIPTING LANGUAGES LIKE PHP, DATABASE MANAGEMENT WITH MYSQL, AND FRAMEWORKS LIKE BOOTSTRAP AND JQUERY. ADDITIONALLY, THE COURSE MAY COVER ADVANCED TOPICS SUCH AS RESPONSIVE WEB DESIGN, VERSION CONTROL SYSTEMS LIKE GIT, AND DEPLOYING WEB APPLICATIONS.

NETWORKING COURSE BY CISCO

THE NETWORKING COURSE OFFERED BY CISCO IS TAILORED TO PROVIDE IN-DEPTH KNOWLEDGE AND PRACTICAL SKILLS IN NETWORKING TECHNOLOGIES. PARTICIPANTS WILL LEARN ABOUT NETWORKING FUNDAMENTALS, INCLUDING TCP/IP, OSI MODEL, ROUTING, SWITCHING, AND SUBNETTING. THEY WILL EXPLORE CISCO'S NETWORKING DEVICES AND TECHNOLOGIES, SUCH AS ROUTERS, SWITCHES, FIREWALLS, AND WIRELESS NETWORKS. THE COURSE MAY ALSO COVER CONFIGURATION, TROUBLESHOOTING, AND OPTIMIZATION OF NETWORK INFRASTRUCTURES, ALONG WITH SECURITY PRINCIPLES AND BEST PRACTICES.

SOLAR INSTALLATION COURSE BY NSDC

THIS COURSE FOCUSES ON TRAINING INDIVIDUALS IN THE INSTALLATION, MAINTENANCE, AND TROUBLESHOOTING OF SOLAR ENERGY SYSTEMS. PARTICIPANTS WILL LEARN ABOUT THE FUNDAMENTALS OF SOLAR ENERGY, INCLUDING PHOTOVOLTAIC (PV) TECHNOLOGY, SOLAR PANELS, INVERTERS, AND BATTERY STORAGE SYSTEMS. THEY WILL ACQUIRE PRACTICAL SKILLS IN SITE ASSESSMENT, SYSTEM DESIGN, INSTALLATION PROCEDURES, ELECTRICAL WIRING, AND SAFETY PROTOCOLS. ADDITIONALLY, THE COURSE MAY COVER REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS, INDUSTRY STANDARDS, AND ENVIRONMENTAL CONSIDERATIONS RELATED TO SOLAR INSTALLATIONS.
